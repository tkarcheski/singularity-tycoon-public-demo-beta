# v0.3 — Leverage, Wear & Entropy (design spec)

Approved 2026-06-12. Builds on `v0.2-jobs-happiness` (jobs/sentiment untouched; all modifiers stack).

## Problem

v0.2 made the economy winnable but the mid-game stalls: after the starter base
(plant + cooler + 3×gpu1, ≈+$1.5/s) the player waits. v0.3 replaces waiting with
decisions: leverage to expand, research with fragility tradeoffs, equipment wear
and repair logistics, and entropy that pushes back as compute scales.

## Systems

### 1. Condition & wear
- Grid cells become `{ t: 'gpu1', cond: 100 }` (null = empty).
- Wear per second per class (`wear` in TILE_TYPES): gpu 0.42, cooler 0.25,
  power 0.18, botbay 0.12, desk/retrain 0.08 — × `1.6^techLevel(class)` ×
  `(1 + 2·entropy01)`; GPUs adjacent to a working cooler wear ×0.5.
- `cond < 40`: **worn** — output ×0.6, amber condition bar.
- `cond ≤ 0`: **broken** — no output/supply, upkeep ×0.5, red ✕; repairs only.
- **Repair tool** (key 8): cost = `0.30 × buildCost × damage01`, restores to 100.

### 2. Research (global tech tracks)
Palette section with three tracks, levels 0→2 (shown I→III):
- Power $600/$3,000 · Cooling $500/$2,500 · Compute $800/$4,000.
- Each level: that class's output ×1.4, wear ×1.6. Applies to all current and
  future tiles of the class.

### 3. Adjacency relationships
- GPU next to N working GPUs (orthogonal): compute ×(1 + 0.10·min(N,3)).
- GPU next to ≥1 working cooler: wear ×0.5.
- Tooltips on placed tiles show condition and active bonuses.

### 4. Bot Bay tile
$350, draws 2 MW, +1 job, upkeep 0.8, wear 0.12. Every 8 ticks (4 s) repairs the
most-damaged other tile by +15 cond, auto-paying 60% of the manual rate
(skips if cash < price). Key 7 (retrain moves to 6, bulldoze to 9).

### 5. Finance — loans + compute futures
- **Loans** (one active at a time): $1k→repay $1.3k, $5k→$6.75k, $25k→$35k.
  Repaid automatically from `max(10% of gross revenue, $0.5/s)`; debt in HUD.
- **Compute futures** (unlock ≥50 TFLOPS, one active): advance =
  `0.75 × revPerSec × 120`; afterwards 50% of gross revenue is withheld until
  `advance/0.75` is delivered.

### 6. Entropy
- `entropy01 = 1 − exp(−totalCompute/150)`; HUD shows 0–100.
- Multiplies wear (above) and rolls events each tick with
  `p = 0.06 × entropy01^1.5`, choosing among events whose preconditions you own
  (more stuff → more failure modes):
  - **Power surge** (≥2 plants): random plant −30 cond.
  - **Coolant leak** (≥1 cooler): cooler −25, adjacent GPUs −10.
  - **Driver crash** (gpu2 or Compute≥II): one GPU offline 8 s.
  - **Brownout** (entropy >70): all GPU output ×0.8 for 10 s.
  - **Bot glitch** (≥1 bay, entropy >50): one bay offline 10 s.
- Events damage and disrupt; they never instantly brick (cond floor 5 from events).

### 7. God-mode dev panel
Collapsible DEV panel (bottom-left), each option an independent toggle:
free build (no cost/permits) · no wear · no entropy (meter 0, no events) ·
pin sentiment (75) · 5× speed (sim time scale) · "+$10k" button.
State on `window.__god`; `window.__state` keeps full sim access.

## Progression arc (target: $1M in 15–25 min)
starter base +$1.5/s → $1k loan → 2nd cluster + desks ≈$5/s → research +
futures → gpu2 farm + bot bays → entropy siege → goal.

## Out of scope (future roadmap, per design discussion)
Multiple datacenter floors; manufacturing chain (GPU factories ← chips ← fabs);
the empire starts small.

## Verification
Playwright over file:// and http://: build flows, wear visibly decays condition,
repair restores it, loans/futures settle correctly, research applies multipliers,
entropy events fire at high compute, god toggles isolate each system, zero
console errors. Manual playtest for feel.
